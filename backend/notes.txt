* Query to get the untranslated keys :
    (select distinct key from i18n_translationkey) except (select distinct a.key from i18n_translationkey a, i18n_translationtext b where a.id = b.key_id);

    $ createdb migratis
    $ createuser -P -S -D -R migratis
